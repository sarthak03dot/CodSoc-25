About CodSoc'25

CodSoc'25 is the vibrant coding society of NITRA Technical Campus, Ghaziabad. We are a passionate community of students united by our love for coding and all things tech.

Our goal is to foster a collaborative environment where coders of all levels — from beginners to advanced — can improve their skills through hands-on workshops, hackathons, coding challenges, seminars, and real-world projects.

We are driven by curiosity and problem-solving. Whether you dream of competing in international contests like ICPC, building software products, or mastering algorithms for interviews — CodSoc'25 is your launchpad.

Join us on this exciting journey, and be part of a community shaping the future, one line of code at a time.
